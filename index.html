About

I am currently a 2016 MBA candidate at Simon Business School (University of Rochester) with a dual concentration in competitive strategy and finance and would like to be where the two subject intersect. I have a deep understanding of the capital markets and had the opportunity to showcase my knowledge this summer. Prior to starting my MBA this year, I won the Fidelity Stock Pitch Competition at the MBA JumpStart conference and I participated in a Private Equity Boot Camp which where I pitched an LBO of Pfizer.

My other interests include community development, entrepreneurship and athletics. I founded NAYP a non-for-profit that promotes financial literacy and college readiness for underserved high school students which I continue to run. I trained Muay Thai and ran Varsity track and basketball.
